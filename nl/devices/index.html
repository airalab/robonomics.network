Robonomics hardware

Meet smart devices & futuristic hardware from an autonomous team with extensive expertise in robotics, blockchain, and IoT.

1

Altruist outdoor sensor

A smart sensor that collects environmental data - noise, dust, and temperature, and stores them on a decentralized sensor map.

Use case:

Become a provider of environmental data to your friends and neighbors in the area. Access truly decentralized measurements of air quality, noise, temperature, and other environmental conditions on the sensor map.

2

Risc-v open source server

The most open-source smart home server, powered by RISC-V, with a Web3 cloud replacing Google servers under the hood.

Use case:

Smart WI-FI MQTT devices with open-source firmware Tasmota on the board fully compatible with Home Assistant.

3

Hikikomori smart Tamagotchi

Finally, a smart Tamagotchi that’s more than just a game! A smart wearable station that checks the status of sensors from your home or clothing.

Use case:

Connect your smart home and smart clothing to the Hikikomori to monitor sensor data—right at your fingertips. Literally.

4

Safe on a smart contract

A safe that unlocks with a smart contract based on user-defined conditions.

Use case:

View logs of the safe’s openings and closings. Program the safe to suit your needs, such as granting access to trusted accounts after an extended period of inactivity.
