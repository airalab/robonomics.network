The number of participants in the program is limited

For content makers

What you may do:
  • 1) work with technical documentation, instructions and tutorials;
  • 2) translate content into your native language;
  • 3) make scientific papers based on Robonomics technologies;
  • 4) popularize ideas of Robonomics.
How:
  • 1) make pull requests on GitHub for website or wiki ;
  • 2) discuss what we need to translate in ambassador chat;
  • 3) create video and text content, try to make it unique for your followers;
  • 4) share progress with other ambassadors and team.

For community helpers

What you may do:
  • 1) organize local offline meetups and hackathons based on Robonomics technologies;
  • 2) participate in thematic conferences on behalf of Robonomics;
  • 3) work online with community: online meetups and hackathons, chat administration, forum moderation, various social networks;
  • 4) communicate with other projects for collaboration.
How:
  • 1) make your suggestion in ambassador’s chat;
  • 2) get merchandise if needed for offline events;
  • 3) share progress with other ambassadors and team.
